Welcome to Case Clicker

About the Game

Case Clicker is an idle game inspired by CS:GO's case opening system. Start with $1000 and build your inventory through various game modes:

🖱️ Clicker

Click to earn money and upgrade your clicking power. Enable auto-clicker and other upgrades to earn passive income.

📦 Cases

Open CS:GO-style cases for a chance to get rare skins. From common blues to rare knives, build your dream inventory!

🎰 Casino

Try your luck with various casino games including Coinflip, Crash, Roulette, Blackjack, and Jackpot.

📈 Progress

Level up from Silver I to Global Elite as you play. Each rank brings new achievements and bragging rights!
